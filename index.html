WELLNESS TIPS

Daily practices for a healthier mind

Evidence-based techniques curated by our specialists.

Prioritise Sleep Hygiene

A consistent sleep schedule regulates your circadian rhythm. Aim for 7–9 hours.

Box Breathing for Anxiety

Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4.

Morning Light Exposure

10–15 minutes of natural light within an hour of waking.

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell and 1 you taste.

Gratitude Journalling

Write down three things you are grateful for every evening.

Social Connection

Schedule one meaningful conversation every week.
